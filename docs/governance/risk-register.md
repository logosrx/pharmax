This register is the standing list of identified risks to Pharmax, the current controls that address them, the residual rating, and the named owner. It is maintained continuously and reviewed formally during the [annual risk assessment](./risk-assessment-procedure.md). Each entry maps to one or more controls in [`../security/control-matrix.md`](../security/control-matrix.md) and, where the residual rating is composite-16 or higher, to an active mitigation plan referenced in the entry.

## Scoring

| Axis       | 1                                                      | 2                                                                | 3                                                                  | 4                                                              | 5                                                                                                |
| ---------- | ------------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Likelihood | Negligible — has not happened in the industry recently | Low — possible in the industry, unlikely against Pharmax in 12 m | Moderate — known events in the industry; possible against Pharmax  | High — recurring industry pattern; plausible against Pharmax   | Very high — recurring against products of our shape; expected if controls degrade                |
| Impact     | Trivial — operational nuisance, no customer impact     | Low — single-customer minor disruption                           | Moderate — multi-customer disruption or single-tenant PHI exposure | Significant — multi-tenant PHI exposure, regulatory engagement | Severe — wide PHI breach, existential reputational damage, regulatory action against the company |

Composite = likelihood × impact. The triage threshold is **composite ≥ 16** — those entries carry an active mitigation plan. Composite 8–15 are monitored and addressed in the next planning cycle. Composite ≤ 7 are accepted with the current controls and re-checked at the annual review.

## Maintenance

- The CTO is the named owner for every entry. As the team grows, ownership rotates to the role best positioned to monitor and address the risk.
- New risks identified during incidents, drills, or vendor reviews are added here within five business days of identification.
- Risks are not removed; they are closed with a residual rating change and the reason. A closed entry remains for the audit trail.
- The annual risk assessment ([`risk-assessment-procedure.md`](./risk-assessment-procedure.md)) is the formal re-rating pass.
